### Book Meetings

The primary goal of this play is to book meetings at the provided calendar link.

You can connect a scheduling tool (Calendly or Cal.com) in `Settings` -> `Integrations`
- With a calendar connected, prospects will be marked won when they book a meeting. 
- Without a calendar connected, prospects will be marketd won when they click your calendar linl.


### Responses

The primary goal of this play is to make the prospect reply with interest.

Once a prospect responds, they will be unenrolled from followup outreach in this Play.


### Link Clicks

The primary goal of this play is to make the prospect click a link in the email.

Once a prospect responds, they will be unenrolled from followup outreach in this Play.

## Select An Offering

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="https://60ed85ec29557f7bbc07a3ef5e23416c.cdn.bubble.io/f1727237066049x785128600749659100/offering.jpg"
/>

Create or select an offering to instruct your Play what you are selling and how to best pitch it.

It's important to describe your product, selling points, and proof points in a clear and succinct way.

You can edit offerings on both the `Training` page or within a specific `Play Editor` page
