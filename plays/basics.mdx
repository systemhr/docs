## Select A Goal

### Book Meetings

The primary goal of this play is to book meetings at the provided calendar link.

If you connect a calendar in Settings, prospects will be marked won when they book a meeting. If you don't have a calendar connected, prospects will be marketd won when they click your calendar linl.


### Responses

The primary goal of this play is to make the prospect reply with interest.


### Link Clicks

The primary goal of this play is to make the prospect click a link in the email.




## Select An Offering

Create or select an offering to instruct your Play what you are selling and how to best pitch it.

It's important to describe your product, selling points, and proof points in a clear and succinct way.
