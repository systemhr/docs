## Outreach Strategy

Strategies instruct your AI rep on how to write email sequences. They contain special instructions for initial emails and each followup email, a specific followup cadence, and training examples.

## Special Writing Instructions

Give special instructions to your AI sales rep while writing certain types of emails. This can be an effective way to modify the output.

Example uses:
- "Always use Australian spelling."
- "Never promise specific timelines for our client projects."
